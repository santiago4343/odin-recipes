Eggs

This potato soup is low calorie with some protein added in order to promote muscle growth and not much fat gain. This, however, makes the soup a little less tasty.

Ingredients:

  • Chicken - 84g
  • Water - ~1000g
  • Salt - 1tbsp
  • Potato - 420g
  • Mushroom - 140g

Steps:

  • Boil water
  • Add salt to water
  • Boil potatoes until cooked
  • Pour potatoes and water to a plate with mushrooms and Chicken
  • Wait until it cools and devour
